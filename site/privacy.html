Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Studystreak is an offline-first app. All data you enter — check-in entries, categories, notes, and values — is stored locally on your device using the file system. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. We do not operate any servers that receive your app data.

What we collect

Nothing. Studystreak does not use analytics SDKs, does not use advertising SDKs, and does not send your data to us or to any third party.

In-App Purchases

Subscription and purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never see your payment details. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Your data

Because everything is stored locally, deleting the app deletes your data. There is no account, login, or cloud sync.
